The Team

After a great pre-season the Avoca Mens senior squad are looking more determined and stronger than ever. Thanks to the fundraising efforts from the rest of the club the Senior team has been attending a fitness course to get them back in shape after summer and ready to do some damage. This year the team sheet has a few new faces, some young, some experienced regardless the coaches believe they are looking better than ever.

The Avoca Vet Mark Cullen (pictured right) with the help of Simon Lowry have made it their mission to build a squad they can rely on and build on for the future. Coming into the 2017 season the team is beginning to shape up as so...

GK: Stephan Gallagher LB: Oisin oSullivan CB: Kevin Mellott SW: Adam Pritchard RB: Eoghan Mellott LM: Andy Meagher CM: Simon Lowry RM: Jonny McCormack
ATT: Daz Hibbert/ Ruiri Nolan / Jonathan Elmes(Elmo).

Club News & Recent Fixtures

Last weekend the mens 1st squad took on Trinity College in their first time meeting this season. Last year in both fixtures TCD were victorious over Avoca 3-2, If Trinity thought they would continue their streak into three games they were sorely mistaken thanks to the squad Mens coach Mark Cullen decided to field. After what we thought was going to be a 1-1 stalemate between the teams Jonny McCormack stole the vivtory in a gripping last minute goal.
