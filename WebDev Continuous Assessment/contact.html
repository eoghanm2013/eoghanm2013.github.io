Please provide information below if your interested in our Newsletter!

As part of our plan to increase support and the size of our club we have started a new inniative to bring up to date information to everyone in the club that tailors to everyone. By providing the information and ticking the boxes relevent to you we can give you the information in the form of a newsletter that applies to you. We will have videos and pictures form various matches sent around via email and first hand post match blogs written by the coaches about their thoughts on the game. Of course if you are a parent of a child in one of our youth squads we will be including information relating to your children such as time and dates of hockey blitzes.
